Eyes of Minerva

High concept:

Eyes of Minerva is a stealth first person puzzle game, where the player will traverse a maze of rooms riddled with enemies looking to eliminate the player. The players will have to use all the tools at their disposal to either distract or disable the enemies



Number of members in the team:

6 programmers / 4 artists



Technology:

Unreal Engine 4



Contribution:

As a gameplay programmer and level designer, my contribution where:

  • Developed the turret enemy and its different variations.
  • Contributed on sound implementations.
  • Contributed on the implementation of the boss.
  • Contributed on the implementation of the boss room.
  • Designed the last level before the boss fight.
